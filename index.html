🏮 Logo Anatomy & Symbolism

Every element in the CONFEST mark tells a story of Pampanga's identity.

🏮 Lantern (Parol)

The central lantern represents the Giant Lantern Festival of San Fernando — the cultural heart of Pampanga. It symbolizes celebration, community, and the "light" CONFEST brings to the local economy.

🚪 Gateway Arch

The outer arch frames the lantern like a doorway — directly referencing the USP: "The Gateway to Pampanga's Economy." It represents entry, access, and the convergence of services.

🌿 Leaf Accent

The organic leaf element signals CONFEST's climate-smart, eco-friendly DNA — weather-responsive bookings, waste management, and the Green Tech Fee that funds sustainability.

⚡ Convergence Nodes

Three small dots around the icon represent the three convergent services: B2B logistics, B2C marketplace, and Climate-Smart booking — the "Super App" integration.

🔤 Split Wordmark

CON (Connect/Commerce) in fire gradient + FEST (Festival/Festivity) in eco gradient. The color split communicates both the business engine and cultural celebration.

✏️ Typography

Cinzel Decorative for the wordmark — a serif display face that evokes heritage, authority, and premium positioning. The "Digital Landlord" energy investors expect.

📐 Usage Guidelines

Minimum clear space, sizing, and do/don't rules.

✅ Do

Use the full-color logo on white or dark backgrounds. Maintain clear space equal to the lantern icon width on all sides. Use the icon-only mark at sizes below 120px width.

✅ Do

Use the monochrome version for single-color print (receipts, stamps, embossing). Use the fire variant for festival/commerce contexts and eco variant for sustainability contexts.

❌ Don't

Never rotate, stretch, or warp the logo. Never place the full-color logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a container. Never change the gradient direction.

❌ Don't

Never separate the leaf from the lantern. Never use the fire palette for eco-specific materials or vice versa. Never reduce below 16px for the icon mark.
